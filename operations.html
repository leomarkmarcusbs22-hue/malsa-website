Malsa Palm Oil Mill (POM)

Our palm oil mill at Man Choon Estate processes fresh fruit bunches into crude palm oil (CPO) and palm kernel. Licensed under MPOB (No. 500266-704000), the mill receives FFB from multiple sources with full traceability.

FFB Collecting Operations

Our three collecting sites — Mile 21, Mile 12, and Matanggar — serve as strategic hubs for FFB purchasing and logistics across the Labuk-Sugut and Beluran districts.

Insert image

Mile 21 (Batu 21)

Our primary FFB collecting site, equipped with a full weighbridge, loading ramp, and gen set facilities. Mile 21 handles significant volumes of FFB from surrounding estates, dealers, and smallholders. The site features dedicated transport logistics to ensure timely delivery of FFB to Malsa POM for processing.

Mile 12 (Batu 12)

Strategically positioned along Jalan Labuk, Mile 12 serves as an efficient midpoint collection hub. The site is equipped with modern weighing systems and streamlined loading infrastructure, enabling quick turnaround of incoming FFB from estates and smallholders across the surrounding area.

Matanggar

Our largest collecting operation by FFB volume. Matanggar handles comprehensive purchasing and transport services, supported by a full complement of office, quarters, and safety infrastructure. The site plays a critical role in ensuring continuous FFB supply flow to the mill throughout the harvest cycle.
